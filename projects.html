A-Level BlackJack Coursework Page

Blackjack Coursework - In Progress

  • This project is a variation the game blackjack, with less stakes and consequences compared to in casinos and online betting websites with real money. Gambling addiction is hugely present in society, and of which the effects of this can be unimaginable. Gambling addiction is a psychological problem which is almost impossible to give up without the help of others. Therefore, this game has been designed to help people suffering with addiction, and with my project I can provide a game where the excitement and euphoric feelings are still present, but without the soul crushing consequences that occur if the cards go the other way. This virtual game with all the same rules, flow of play and design is perfect for this problem, using a fake, virtual currency so the player can still get the satisfaction of seeing their balance rising, but without the catastrophic financial losses that occur in real life if the cards don't turn out the way they want them to.
